QUANTITES AND SMALL ORDER FEES

Invitation quantities of 50 or above must be ordered in increments of 5. We recommend ordering one invitation per household (not per guest) plus an extra 10-15% to cover postal errors, last minute additions and keepsakes. We have a 20-invite minimum for any re-orders.

Orders between a quantity of 10 and 49 will incur a Small Order Fee, but can be ordered in any increment.

INK COLOR OPTIONS

Select Color

All ink colors print in a matte finish. If selecting your own colors, we recommend choosing at least 1 neutral shade to balance and accent vibrant colors.

Due to monitor limitations, your invitations may not exactly match your screen. Our sample invitations include a printed color chart to reference actual colors.

Custom Ink Color: $25

Can’t find the best color match? Custom ink colors are available, although a physical color swatch or specific Pantone color code will be required in order to properly formulate the best shade.

RSVP Styles

3.5” x 5” RSVP Postcard with Printed Return Address
Reply card that is printed with your return address on the back of the card. Your guests will mail this back to you without an envelope. Great for the budget shopper since it only requires a Postcard Stamp instead of a First Class Stamp!

3.5” x 5” RSVP Single Sided Card with Blank Envelope
Formal reply card with a blank envelope in the color of your choice. Handwrite, stamp, print or label your return address on the front of your envelope. We recommend pre-stamping your envelopes with a First Class stamp for your guest’s convenience.

3.5” x 5” RSVP Single Sided Card with Printed Return Address on Envelope
Formal reply card and envelope with your printed return address on the front. Envelope colors and fonts can coordinate with your invitation design. We recommend pre-stamping these with a First Class stamp for your guest’s convenience.

Replace RSVP Card
If you do not require a mail-in response, substitute your reply card with any insert of your choosing. This card can be used for important details such as a website for your guests to reply, reception or travel information, accommodations, etc. Simply pay the difference in cost between the two cards if additional size fees are required.

Alternative RSVP Versions

Considering multiple RSVP cards? Here are some examples why additional RSVP versions may be necessary for your event:

You’re having a small intimate ceremony but a larger reception. Some guests will receive an RSVP to both events and others will only receive an RSVP to the reception.
The Wedding Party and/or out of town guests are traditionally invited to the pre and post wedding events. Multiple RSVP versions would be required. One version for guests only invited to the wedding and another for guests invited to the wedding and any additional events.
Occasionally, some events require multiple RSVP dates. In this instance, you would need multiple versions with different reply dates.

Your final card quantities will be due once your digital proof design has been approved.

Insert Cards

3.5” x 5” Insert Card (RSVP Size)
Being the same size as our RSVP Card, it is perfect if you do not need a tiered card look and wish to include all of your important details on the same size card! Great for rehearsal or brunch details, additional events, or website information.

3.75” x 3.75” Small Insert Card - Up to 5 lines of text
Typically used for reception details, dress attire, or wedding website which may include other event information where guests can reply.

4.75” x 3.75” Medium Insert Card - Up to 7 lines of text, Vertical and Horizontal
Typically used for any pre and/or post weekend events such as a brunch, luncheon, rehearsal or shabbat dinner.

5.75” x 3.75” Large Insert Card - Up to 10 lines of text, Vertical and Horizontal
Perfect for out of town guests! Can include hotel and accommodations information or any other travel or event details you need your guests to know.

Embellishments

Pocketfold Name Tag:
Adhered to the front flap of your Pocketfold, providing a beautiful first impression when your guests remove the invitation from their mailing envelope! This tag is printed on the same cardstock that you have selected for your invitation and includes your name(s) or initials and event date.

Pre-Punched Name Tag:
Printed with your names(s) or initials and event date on the same cardstock that you select for your invitation. Arriving with a hole punch and ready for easy assembly, you can purchase a beautiful ribbon of your choosing to finish off the invitation!

Cardstock Belly Band:
Printed with your name(s) or initials and event date on the same cardstock that you select for your invitation, this band wraps snugly around your invitation suite, creating a clean and modern finishing touch!

Hanging Name Tag:
Beautifully enclose all components of your suite with a personalized name tag and your choice or ribbon, raffia or twine! Printed on the same cardstock that you have selected for your invitation with your name(s) or initials and event date.

Invitation Wrapping Only:
Not in need of a personalized tag and prefer a simple wrapping on its own? Our ribbon, raffia or twine wrapping can beautifully enclose all components of your suite! Raffia or Twine will provide your invitation with a modern, natural or rustic appearance while our ⅞” Satin Ribbon gives your suite a more formal and romantic feel.

Envelope Addressing

Digital Calligraphy

Addresses are printed directly on envelopes using the font and ink color matching your invitation design! As we are only able to print in darker ink colors, digital calligraphy is only available for lighter colored envelopes to ensure the legibility of the addressing.

Unsure of how to properly address your invitations? We provide you with an Excel Spreadsheet to submit your guest list so that all addresses are printed in the correct format. You will receive a digital proof showing each envelope prior to printing.

Address Wraps

A strip of paper with the coordinating paper texture that is permanently adhered around your mailing envelope. Your Return Address Only or Guest’s Names and Addresses are printed on each wrap in fonts and ink colors that coordinate with your invitation design!

Unsure of how to properly address your invitations? We provide you with an Excel Spreadsheet to submit your guest list so that all addresses are printed in the correct format. You will receive a digital proof showing each envelope prior to printing.
