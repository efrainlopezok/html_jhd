about Julie Hanan Design

Julie Hanan Design is a premier online stationery and graphic design studio specializing in modern wedding invitations, bar or bat mitzvah invitations, and custom business materials. A collection of stationery items including Programs, Place Cards, Thank You Cards and so much more are available to coordinate with your special event. All invitations, stationery and business items are printed on the finest quality cardstocks and hand assembled in-house so you can be assured of the quality and care of each item. Our goal is to bring your unique vision to life with our collection of modern, classic, rustic, vintage, destination and romantic designs!
