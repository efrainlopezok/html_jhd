INVITATION QUANTITY

Invitation quantities of 50 or above must be ordered in increments of 5. We recommend ordering one invitation per household (not per guest) plus an extra 10-15% to cover postal errors, last minute additions and keepsakes. We have a 20-invite minimum for any re-orders.

Quantities between 10 and 49 will incur a Small Order Fee, but can be ordered in any increment. Minimum quantity of 10.

INVITATION SIZE

Postage cost will be based on the size, thickness and weight of the invitation. We recommend taking your invitation to your local post office to receive an accurate estimate

Please note that 6”x 6” invitations will require additional postage.

INVITATION STYLES

Flat Panel styles are offered with up to two accent layers of cardstock to provide a beautiful border for your main card.

POCKETFOLD INVITATION STYLES

Pocketfolds are a tri-fold card with a pocket to enclose your RSVP and/or Insert Cards. They can be used with or without an accent layer!

NOT SURE LET US HELP

To help us pick the perfect colors, please provide inspiration images including flowers, bridesmaid dresses, linens or other decor elements. Color selections can be changed anytime before you approve your digital proof.

INVITATION INK COLORS

All ink colors print in a matte finish. We recommend choosing at least 1 neutral shade to balance and accent vibrant colors.

Due to monitor limitations, your invitations may not exactly match your screen. Our sample invitations include a printed color chart to reference actual colors.

CHOOSE A CUSTOM INK COLOR

Can’t find the best color match? Custom ink colors are available, although a physical color swatch or specific Pantone color code will be required in order to properly formulate the best shade.

  • Color choice will be determined before invitation proof has been designed.

DOUBLE SIDED RSVP POSTCARD

Reply card that is printed with your return address on the back of the card. Your guests will mail this back to you without an envelope and only a Postcard Stamp is required instead of a First Class Stamp! Great for the budget shopper!

SINGLE SIDED RSVP CARD & ENVELOPE

Formal reply card with a envelope in the color of your choice. Optional Return Address Printing to coordinate with your invitation design is available. We recommend pre-stamping your envelopes with a First Class stamp for your guest’s convenience.

REPLACE RSVP CARD

If you do not require a mail-in response, substitute your reply card with any insert of your choosing. This card can be used for important details such as a website for your guests to reply, reception or travel information, accommodations, etc. Simply pay the difference in cost between the two cards if additional size fees are required.

STAMPING SERVICE

Let us save you time by pre-stamping your RSVP Postcards! For only the cost of postage plus a small fee, our Stamping Service provides one less thing you have to worry about since your invitations will arrive fully assembled.

STAMPING SERVICE

Let us save you time by pre-stamping your RSVP Envelopes! For only the cost of postage plus a small fee, our Stamping Service provides one less thing you have to worry about since your invitations will arrive fully assembled.

ALTERNATE RSVP VERSIONS

Considering multiple RSVP cards? Here are some examples why additional RSVP versions may be necessary for your event:

  1. You’re having a small intimate ceremony but a larger reception. Some guests will receive an RSVP to both events and others will only receive an RSVP to the reception.

  2. The Wedding Party and/or out of town guests are traditionally invited to the pre and post wedding events. Multiple RSVP versions would be required. One version for guests only invited to the wedding and another for guests invited to the wedding and any additional events.

  3. Occasionally, some events require multiple RSVP dates. In this instance, you would need multiple versions with different reply dates.

Your final card quantities will be due once your digital proof design has been approved.

Extra RSVP Cards

Extra RSVP cards without envelopes can be ordered if you are unsure on the exact quantity you need.

PAPER SELECTIONS

STANDARD CARDSTOCKS
100# LINEN textured

Lightest weight cardstock with a crosshatch texture appearance that allows for crisp printing. Not recommended for our Flat Panel, No Accent Layer invitation style.

130# SMOOTH matte

Heavy weight cardstock with a clean smooth, matte finish allowing for very crisp and saturated printing. Sturdy enough to be used without an accent layer or pocketfold backing.

130# SIGNATURE textured

Heavy weight cardstock with a dimpled texture finish that allows for crisp, saturated printing. Sturdy enough to be used without an accent layer or pocketfold backing.

 

LUXURY CARDSTOCKS
111# CREAM LUXE textured

Heavy weight Italian cotton cardstock with a luxurious textured surface. Printed ink colors will absorb into the paper, producing a stunning marbled effect. Sturdy enough to be used without an accent layer or pocketfold backing.

137# PEARL WHITE metallic

Our heaviest cardstock with a smooth, shimmer finish and will give all printed ink a crisp and saturated, pearlescent appearance. Sturdy enough to be used without an accent layer or pocketfold backing.

POCKETFOLD NAME TAG

Adhered to the front flap of your Pocketfold, providing a beautiful first impression when your guests remove the invitation from their mailing envelope! This tag is printed on the same cardstock that you have selected for your invitation and includes your name(s) or initials and event date.

PRE-PUNCHED NAME TAG

Printed with your names(s) or initials and event date on the same cardstock that you select for your invitation. Arriving with a hole punch and ready for easy assembly, you can purchase a beautiful ribbon of your choosing to finish off the invitation!

PRINTED BELLY BAND

Printed with your name(s) or initials and event date on the same cardstock that you select for your invitation. This band wraps snugly around your invitation, enclosing your suite, to create a clean and modern finishing touch!

RIBBON WRAPPING

Not in need of a personalized tag and prefer a simple wrapping on its own? Our ⅞” Satin Ribbon wrapping can beautifully enclose all components of your suite, giving it a formal and romantic feel.

HANGING NAME TAG

MONOGRAM TAG WITH WRAPPING

Beautifully enclose all components of your suite with a layered name tag and your choice of metallic or glitter wrap! The tag is printed on the same cardstock that you have selected for your invitation with your name(s) or initials and event date.

Envelope Addressing

Digital Calligraphy

Addresses are printed directly on envelopes using the font and ink color matching your invitation design! As we are only able to print in darker ink colors, digital calligraphy is only available for lighter colored envelopes to ensure the legibility of the addressing.

Address Wraps

A strip of lightweight paper that coordinates with your invitation cardstock and is permanently adhered around your mailing envelope. Available with either your Return Address Only or Guest & Return Address. Each wrap is printed in fonts and ink colors that coordinate with your invitation design!

Unsure of how to properly address your invitations? We provide you with an Excel Spreadsheet to submit your guest list so that all addresses are printed in the correct format. You will receive a digital proof showing each envelope prior to printing.

DESIGN TURNAROUND

A digital proof of your invitation, including 3 rounds of revisions, will be emailed to you. The time it takes to proof is entirely up to you, and once approved and paid for we will begin printing.

Order details and wording must be confirmed by 3pm Central time or your turnaround time will begin on the next business day.

PRODUCTION TURNAROUND

Once your design has been approved and paid for, we will send your invitations to print! Your order will be will be assembled to your specifications within your chosen production turnaround time. If your order requires no assembly, or you have selected to fully assemble the invitation yourself, we will automatically upgrade you to the next production tier, free of charge!

Invitation approval and payment must be made by 12pm Central Time or your production time will begin on the next business day.
