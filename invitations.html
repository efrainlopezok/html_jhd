INVITATIONS 101

Not sure what’s in an invitation? We’re here to help!
Personalize any of our designs with your unique color scheme, wording and embellishments!

the invitation

quantity

We recommend ordering one invitation per household
(not guests) plus an extra 10-15% to cover postal errors,
last minute additions and keepsakes!

size

Our invitations come in 5"x7" and 6"x6" sizes. Please note that the 6"x6" invitations will require additional postage. Postage costs are determined on the size, thickness and weight of the invitation. We recommend taking an invitation to your local post office to receive an accurate estimate.

main invitation card

Include key details for your special day! Most invitations include the host names, guest(s) of honor, time, date, year and ceremony location.

FLAT PANEL STYLE

NO ACCENT LAYERS

1 ACCENT LAYER

2 ACCENT LAYERS

Accent layers are additional pieces of cardstock that are used as backing to your main invitation. They are a great way to add depth and distinction to any design allowing your invitation to have a luxurious and creative touch! Up to 2 Accent Layers can be added for Flat Panel Styles and 1 accent layer for Pocketfolds.

POCKETFOLD STYLE

flat panel style

1 ACCENT LAYERS

Similar to a book, this tri-fold card comes folded and opens up to have a pocket on the inside. The main invitation will be adhered to the center section of the pocketfold and additional insert cards can be inserted into the attached pocket, allowing for a clean and organized appearance!

rsvp card

Formal reply cards with a response deadline and place for your guests to write their names and number attending. Additional lines can also be included for meal selections, special dietary needs, other events or a song request! For your guest's convenience, including your return address and postage on an envelope or postcard is recommended. International guests do not need to be pre-stamped since they require country-specific postage.

We also encourage numbering the back side of your response cards according to your guest list. This will make it easier to see who responded in case they forgot to include their name or if their handwriting is too difficult to read.

Multiple RSVP card versions are
available to invite each guest to the
event(s) of your preference!

Use your own labels or hand address
your RSVP envelopes to help lower
the total cost of your invitations without
sacrificing the RSVP envelope completely.

INSERT CARDS

3.75” x 3.75” SMALL INSERT CARD

If your ceremony and reception are located at different places, or on a different day, it is recommended to include a separate insert card inviting your guests to this event. This card can also be used for other small amounts of information such as dress attire, a wedding website, or other important details.

4.75” x 3.75” MEDIUM INSERT CARD

Typically used to include other pre and / or post events such as a luncheon, brunch, sangeet, mehndi, rehearsal dinner, or shabbat dinner. However, feel free to use this card for any important information you need to include!

5.75” x 3.75” LARGE INSERT CARD

Typically used to include other pre and / or post events such as a luncheon, brunch, sangeet, mehndi, rehearsal dinner, or shabbat dinner. However, feel free to use this card for any important information you need to include!

It is important to note that etiquette experts suggest that information regarding your wedding registry or wishing well should not be included with your invitation since it is implies a gift is expected. Traditionally, registry information was given by word of mouth although in modern times it is ok to include this information on a wedding website.

the envelope

colors

Our envelopes are offered in over 60 different colors to ensure you get the custom look you want for no extra cost! Guest and/or Return Addressing options and envelope liners can be added for an additional charge to coordinate with your design.

inner envelopes

Inner envelopes are not included in our base package pricing because we include all invited guest names on the mailing envelope. This helps save on additional postage, addressing and envelope costs. Traditionally, an inner envelope would include the name of every invited guest while the outer (mailing) envelope only includes the head(s) of household. If you prefer the more traditional and formal option, inner envelopes are available for an additional cost.

PATTERNED

Patterned liners provide your suite an upscale feel, ensuring no detail is overlooked! Printed patterns can coordinate or contrast with your design to make a gorgeous statement to your mailing envelope!
